Debunking Common Myths About Gambling

There are persistent myths about gambling that can fuel risky behaviours. It is important to know them:

  • Online gambling is not an investment: The platform always has a statistical advantage in the long term. Gambling to make money is a dangerous mindset, even on trusted sites like jackpotcity casino.
  • No "sure" strategy exists: No tactic or system can overcome the randomness (RNG) of fair slot games. Jackpotcity reviews often emphasise that luck, not strategy, determines outcomes.
  • "Chasing losses" thinking: Losses cannot be recouped by playing longer or more; each round is independent. This concept is known as the "gambler's fallacy".
